About Bharatanatyam

Bharatanatyam is one of India's oldest and most revered classical dance forms, originating in the temples of Tamil Nadu. This divine art form combines intricate footwork (nritta), expressive storytelling (nritya), and spiritual connection (bhava). Through precise hand gestures (mudras), facial expressions (abhinaya), and rhythmic movements, dancers bring to life ancient stories and emotions.
