Bharatanatyam is one of the oldest classical dance forms of India. Originating in Tamil Nadu, this divine art form denotes various emotions through facial expressions and hand gestures, bringing ancient stories to life.

The main item of a Bharatanatyam recital. This complex piece showcases the dancer's technical and expressive abilities through an elaborate blend of nritta (pure dance) and nritya (expressive dance).
